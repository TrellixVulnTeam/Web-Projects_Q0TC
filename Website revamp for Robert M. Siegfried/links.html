Autism

Autism is a developmental disorder that varies in severity and has become increasingly common. While there is no cure, there are therapies that can help enormously in leading an autistic child to a more normal life. The following sites provide information on autism as well as some leads to finding services in the Long Island area:

  • Autism Speaks - An organization recently started by Bob and Suzanne Wright to help find a cure for autism.
  • Center for the Study for Autism - They provide mainly informational resources, but they are very good.
  • Asperger's Disorder home - a page of resources for families and individuals with Aspergers Syndrome, a high-functioning developmental disorder akin to autism
  • Autism Society of America - an organization started by the parents of autistic children hat provides information and resources
  • The Nassau-Suffolk Chapter of the Autism Society
  • Kulanu - an organization within the Orthodox Jewish community of Long Island that makes an enormous difference in the lives of children with physical and developmental disabilities.
  • QSAC - Quality Services for Autism Community, which provides services for autistic children, in New York City and Long Island
